As AI tools get better at writing SQL and basic Python scripts, the role of a Data Engineer is shifting. We are moving away from building bespoke pipelines and moving towards managing platforms, ensuring data quality, and integrating vector databases for LLMs.

<Callout type="info">The best way to future-proof your career is to focus on fundamentals: distributed systems, networking, and data architecture.</Callout>
