Data engineering interviews are notoriously broad. Here is what you should focus on:

1. **SQL**: You must be an expert in window functions, CTEs, and aggregations.
2. **Python**: Focus on basic data structures, generators, and clean code.
3. **System Design**: Be prepared to design an end-to-end pipeline on a whiteboard.
4. **Data Modeling**: Understand Star Schema vs Snowflake, and SCD Types 1, 2, and 3.
