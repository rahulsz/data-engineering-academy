Kafka is a distributed event streaming platform. Think of it as a massive, highly scalable post office.

- **Producers**: People dropping off mail (sending events).
- **Topics**: The mailboxes (where events are stored).
- **Consumers**: People picking up mail (reading events).
- **Partitions**: Splitting a mailbox into multiple slots so multiple people can read at the same time.
