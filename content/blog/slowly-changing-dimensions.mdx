When a customer moves to a new city, do you overwrite their old city in your database? If you do, your past sales reports will retroactively assign their old purchases to their new city!

### SCD Type 2

Slowly Changing Dimension Type 2 solves this by adding new rows instead of updating existing ones. You add `valid_from`, `valid_to`, and `is_current` columns.

```sql
SELECT * FROM dim_customer WHERE is_current = TRUE;
```
